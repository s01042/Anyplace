Some hints

The timeline and map component are mouse and touch-aware. If your device has a touchscreen you can use it.

The components layout is responsive and should work on smaller and larger screens, eg. iPad, different mobile phones and desktop PCs.

You can navigate the timeline by the left and right cursor keys.

You can pinch to zoom and move the map by mouse and swipe gestures.
